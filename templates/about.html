About Us

Welcome to DermaDetect, a platform dedicated to transforming the way skin cancer is detected and managed through cutting-edge technology.

Our Mission

At DermaDetect, we aim to harness the power of artificial intelligence to make early detection of skin cancer accessible, reliable, and accurate for everyone. By leveraging deep learning techniques, we strive to assist healthcare professionals and empower individuals with the tools needed for timely diagnosis and intervention.

The Problem We Address

Skin cancer is the most common form of cancer globally, with millions of new cases reported annually. Early detection significantly improves the chances of successful treatment, yet traditional diagnostic methods often fall short due to limitations such as human error and resource constraints.

Our Solution

DermaDetect uses EfficientNetB0 — a state-of-the-art transfer learning model — trained on over 35,000 dermoscopic images from the HAM10000 and ISIC 2019 datasets. It classifies skin lesions into seven categories with 78% validation accuracy.

  • Advanced Image Analysis: EfficientNetB0 processes full 224×224 resolution images, capturing fine-grained texture and pattern details in skin lesions.
  • Grad-CAM Visualisation: Every diagnosis is accompanied by a heatmap showing exactly which region of the image influenced the model's decision.
  • Class-Weighted Training: Critical classes like Melanoma are boosted during training to reduce missed detections of high-risk lesions.
  • PDF Reports: Clinician-ready downloadable reports including patient info, diagnosis, confidence score, and Grad-CAM imagery.
  • Patient Records: Secure MongoDB-backed history of all scans, searchable and filterable by diagnosis type.

Why Choose Us?

  • Scientific Validation: Our research, published in leading journals, demonstrates the reliability and effectiveness of our model.
  • Focus on Accessibility: Designed to benefit underserved populations, our solution works seamlessly with images captured using everyday devices like smartphones.
  • Commitment to Innovation: By integrating advanced machine learning techniques with dermatological insights, we are paving the way for more accurate and scalable healthcare solutions.

Our Team

DermaDetect is built by a dedicated team of researchers and developers from K.D.K. College of Engineering, Nagpur. Combining expertise in computer science and a passion for healthcare innovation, our team has developed a solution that bridges the gap between technology and medical needs.

Join Us in the Fight Against Skin Cancer

We believe that technology can save lives, and we invite you to explore our platform and be a part of this transformative journey. Whether you're a patient, a healthcare provider, or a researcher, DermaDetect is here to support your efforts in the early detection and management of skin cancer.
